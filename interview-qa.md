### Q1. Explain Functional Testing with an example.
**Answer:** Verifying login, add-to-cart, payment, and order tracking features work as expected.

### Q2. What is Unit Testing in this application?
**Answer:** Testing individual components like price calculation, coupon validation, and login logic.

### Q3. Give an Integration Testing example.
**Answer:** Verifying cart module correctly interacts with payment and order services.

### Q4. What is System Testing?
**Answer:** End-to-end validation from login to order delivery and admin update.

### Q5. Why is UAT critical?
**Answer:** It ensures the system meets business and real user expectations.
